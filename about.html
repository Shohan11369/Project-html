CP FOOD BUSINESS

CP Food Business is one of the three core businesses of CP Bangladesh Co., Ltd. started in 2008 with the slogan of “Food to enrich life”.

CP Food business has 3 major parts:

i.CP Five Star

ii.CP Modern Trade

iii.CP Fresh Chicken

Five Star

To meet the huge customer demand of ready food, CP Food inaugurated its business in Bangladesh since 2008 under the brand name 'CP Five Star' and from the beginning, it has been providing the most hygienic, nutritious, safe and Halal ready food to the customers.

CP Modern Trade

CP Food business inaugurated pack brand of CP Chilled & frozen processed foods in Modern Trade channel in Bangladesh since 2010. Chicken Ball, Sausage, Nuggets, Italian Chili Bologna, Chicken Patty and Frank Cocktail are available in Super Stores like; Shwapno, Meena Bazar, Agora, Unimart, Prince Bazar.

CP Fresh Chicken

C.P. Bangladesh Co., Ltd. is rearing chicken by using own Feed & chicks. Then slaughtering at own slaughter house which is the largest in Bangladesh maintaining EU standard and Halal process recommended by Islamic Foundation. Serving in different channels like Hotels, Super Shops, Café, Restaurants etc.
